When the relationship begins (The Honeymoon Phase), our brain is flooded with dopamine and oxytocin. This biological filter hides the truth. We don't ask "Why do I love them?" We just enjoy the high.

## 1.4 Debugging the Heart (The Solution)
So, are we doomed to suffer? No. We can rewrite the script.

### Protocol A: Stop the Cycle (Safety Valve)
